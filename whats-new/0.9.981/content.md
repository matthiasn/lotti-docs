# January Update

Welcome to the first update of 2026! This release brings exciting new features and improvements.

---

## Timeline Visualization

A new video-editor-style timeline helps you navigate your task backlog with ease. Scroll through time to see your entries laid out chronologically.

- Drag to scroll through days and weeks
- Tap any entry to jump directly to it
- Pinch to zoom for different time scales

---

## Improved Entry Actions

The entry action buttons have been completely redesigned with a cleaner, more intuitive layout.

- Quick actions are now always visible
- New swipe gestures for common tasks
- Better visual feedback on interactions

---

## Performance Improvements

Under the hood, we've made significant performance improvements:

- Faster app startup time
- Smoother scrolling in long lists
- Reduced memory usage
